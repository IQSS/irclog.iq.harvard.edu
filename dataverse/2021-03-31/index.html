16:16 pdurbin Differential privacy is a way to add noise to data. It helps you share summary statistics for data you can't otherwise share. We're working on integrating Dataverse with another project called OpenDP: https://opendp.org
16:17 pdurbin So the idea is that you keep the file restricted in Dataverse but you can share somewhat noisy summary statistics of that data to give people an idea of what's in the data.
